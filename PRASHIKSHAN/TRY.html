Bridge the gap between colleges, students and industry — NEP-aligned internships

Prashiskshan is a lightweight platform to post verified internships, manage credits, auto-generate NEP-compliant logbooks and help students prepare for industry roles.

Student-first

Search & Apply

Filtered internships, offline-friendly view, personalised recommendations.

Industry

Post & Manage

Create internship postings, short-term trials, API-ready dashboards for bulk hiring.

Faculty

Monitor & Credit

Track logbooks, approve credits and validate reports to avoid fakes.

Why Semester 5 internships?

Semester 5 under NEP is ideal for practical exposure — students are academically ready and credit-based internships improve learning outcomes.

Pilot-ready

Stakeholders

  • Industry: postings, dashboards

How it works

Student flow

  1. Create profile & upload resume
  2. Search & apply to internships
  3. Maintain daily logbook (auto-save)
  4. Generate NEP-compliant report & submit for credits

Industry & Faculty

  1. Industry posts internships — short trial slots available
  2. Faculty monitors progress & signs off credits
  3. Analytics used for curriculum improvements

Feature highlights

Auto Logbook

Timestamps, daily work items and supervisor comments create a tamper-evident logbook.

Credit Integration

Faculty can map internship outcomes to course credits with one click.

Offline & SMS

Cached internships, apply offline and queue sync when connectivity returns. SMS alerts for rural students.
